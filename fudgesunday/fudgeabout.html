About me

Welcome to the sonic world of Fudge Sunday, where music isn't just heard, it's felt.

With a lifelong passion for rhythm and melody, I've journeyed through the realms of sound, from the stages of live performances with bands to the digital landscapes of video games and content creation.

My musical odyssey began as a student of music, where I immersed myself in the artistry of various instruments, learning their intricacies and mastering their voices. This foundation has allowed me to craft compositions that are rich in depth and diversity, drawing from a palette of experiences that transcend genres and captivate audiences.

As a seasoned musician, I've honed my skills not just in playing instruments but also in the art of production. This evolution has led me to collaborate with video game developers and content creators, where I breathe life into their projects with bespoke soundtracks that elevate their storytelling.

Join me on this auditory adventure as we explore the boundless possibilities of music. Whether you're seeking the perfect score for your game or a soundtrack that resonates with your audience, together, we'll create harmonies that linger in the hearts and minds of listeners long after the last note fades.
